Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2024

Introduction

History Cleaner by SelfCode ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles your data.

Data Collection and Use

Our extension only accesses your browser history for the sole purpose of deleting it when you click the "Clear History" button. We do not:

  • Collect or store any browsing history data
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

How It Works

When you click the "Clear History" button, the extension uses Chrome's built-in API (chrome.history.deleteAll) to clear your browsing history. This process happens entirely within your browser, and no data is transmitted outside of your device.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • "history" - to clear your browsing history
  • "storage" - to store extension settings locally

Data Retention

We do not retain any data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
